Payment FAQ

Is my payment information secure?

Yes. We use industry-standard encryption and are PCI-DSS compliant. All payment processing is handled by trusted, certified payment processors.

Which payment method should I use?

Choose whichever method is most convenient for you. All methods are equally secure. For international payments, wire transfer or PayPal typically work best.

When will my payment be processed?

Card and digital wallet payments are processed immediately. Bank transfers and peer-to-peer payments (Venmo, Cash App) will be processed within 1-2 business days.

What's your refund policy?

Subscriptions can be cancelled within 7 days for a full refund. Code ownership packages are non-refundable after development begins. See our billing page for details.

Will I receive an invoice?

Yes. A detailed invoice will be sent to your email address immediately after payment is received. You can also request invoices anytime from our team.

What if my payment fails?

You'll see an error message explaining why. Common reasons include insufficient funds, incorrect card details, or network issues. Please try again or use a different payment method.
