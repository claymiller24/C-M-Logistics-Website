Frequently Asked Questions

Can I switch subscription tiers?

Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade anytime. Changes take effect at the next billing cycle. Upgrading starts immediately with prorated charges.

What's the difference between subscription and code ownership?

Subscriptions provide ongoing development services with non-exclusive code. Code ownership is a one-time purchase that gives you full, exclusive ownership of the codebase and all intellectual property rights.

Do you offer custom packages?

Absolutely! Every project is unique. Contact us to discuss custom pricing, hourly rates, or hybrid arrangements that fit your specific needs.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, wire transfers, ACH transfers, and PayPal. Enterprise clients can discuss invoicing and custom payment terms.

Is there a setup fee?

No setup fees! Your first month's billing starts immediately upon subscription. Code ownership purchases are all-inclusive.

What if I need more than my plan's hours?

We offer hourly overages at $125/hour. You'll receive a notification when approaching your monthly limit so you can adjust or upgrade.
